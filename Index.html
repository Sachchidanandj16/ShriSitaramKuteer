About The Trust

The Thakur Shri Hanuman Ji Maharaj Govind Bag Samiti Trust is a non-profit, spiritual organization dedicated to upholding the timeless traditions of Sanatana Dharma. Our core mission revolves around the service (seva) of the sacred Shri Sitaram Kuteer, home to the ever-present Lord Hanuman, in the holy land of Vrindavan.

Our History

Founded with divine inspiration, the Kuteer has been a beacon of spiritual solace for decades. The Trust was formed to formally manage and preserve this sacred heritage for generations to come.

Our Objectives

To ensure the daily worship at the temple, maintain the ashram premises, spread spiritual knowledge through satsang and literature, and engage in selfless community service.
