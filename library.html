The Spiritual Library

A tranquil space for study, reflection, and the pursuit of divine knowledge.

A Treasury of Sacred Texts

Our reading gallery offers a serene and welcoming environment for devotees and seekers to immerse themselves in a vast collection of sacred texts and devotional literature. It is a place to deepen one's understanding of Sanatana Dharma and connect with the timeless wisdom of the saints and sages.

Our Collection

Vishnu SahastraNama

The Vishnu Sahastranama is a Sanskrit hymn that contains a list of 1,000 names of Lord Vishnu.

Epics & Puranas

Complete editions of the Ramayana, Mahabharata, and key Puranas.

Gita & Commentaries

Various translations and detailed commentaries on the Bhagavad Gita.

Biographies of Saints

Inspiring life stories and teachings of great spiritual masters and devotees.
